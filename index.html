About Us


"When China sneezes, Whole world catches cold...."

What are we?
We are sync-x, a crowdsourced initiative, working to improve the status of online education in remote
towns/villages in this unprecedented time. Owing to the challenges faced by schools/colleges, your team
has come up with a software which uses very low bandwidth to stream online content, making it ideal for
places with connectivity issues.



Why are we taking this initiave


“In any given moment we have two options: to step forward into growth or step back into safety...”


2020 the 404 error year, Beggining with such a crisis and now just praying for our lives. Well what we
studied in junior grade that nature always balances in some way or the other and now what we get is stay
home and watch the world save by the heroes who do not wear cape, any suit neither they come with any
dramatic sounds nor any hi-tech weapon, they came with some surgeon mask, hand sanitizer, syringes and
what not, yes they are the doctors and nurses we see in hospital working and giving up their life for us.

Due to this pandemic crisis, schools are trying their best to provide their students education but here
what a problem lies due to such video calls or meetings there is a huge bandwidth and there are some people
who are located at remote areas and do not have stable connections for such facilities and from here our journey started.

Objectives


""Failures arise when you take your aims for granted....""

We all know the online schooling as a solution given by schools to teach their students but since everything is
not easily excuted, there are places or childrens who cannot access this facility and due to this millions of children in
India or other students in world cannot have education because of the problems from the client-side or from
the server-side and some meeting apps are considred as easily breached by hackers and since this is the problem,
here we come in with our aim to give this facilities to these students as soon as possible.
